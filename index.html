We do House.
Soulful, chunky and deep.
A nod to the old with roots in the new.
The kinda music that pulses through your veins.
The kinda music you’ll want to play...to a packed dancefloor or just trippin’ on your own.

Started by South African producer Matt Prehn & delivered by an underground family of
artists across the globe.

Releasing digitally and in physical format since 2010.

We pride ourselves in representing some of the most cutting edge dance music coming
out of South Africa and beyond. For a small once off fee you can receive all of our latest tracks ahead of release date exclusively to your inbox. Full wav and mp3 downloads that includes music from Oh So Coy, Rogue Decibels, Things We Make & The Travelling Bear.

Looking to the future we aim to establish ourselves as a hub for South African labels.
With our incredible distribution network and contacts we'd love to chat further about the opportunity to take you and your label to the next level!!

Simply click below to contact us.
We'd love to help come up with a plan that fits your label ambitions.

Chanwill Maconi is still fairly new to the game but his classic old school sound will have you believing otherwise. 4 Raw analogue inspired originals are on offer moving from dubbed-out to soulful to more Kerri Chandler-esque grooves.

Our 3rd vinyl outing courtesy of German resident Marc Heun. Quality production encapsulates all four tracks that move effortlessly from deep to discofied house utilising excellent sampling techniques.
